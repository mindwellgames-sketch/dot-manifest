# Privacy Policy for .manifest

**Last Updated:** November 23, 2025

## Introduction

This privacy policy describes how .manifest ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **What we collect:** Your approximate geographic location (latitude and longitude)
- **How we use it:**
  - To calculate sunrise and sunset times for displaying time-of-day gradient colors in your daily routines
  - To provide location-based address suggestions when adding locations to tasks and appointments
- **Storage:** Location data is cached locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers
- **Control:** You can manage location permissions through your device's Settings app

### User-Generated Content
- **What we collect:** Your routine items, tasks, appointments, and history entries that you create within the app
- **How we use it:** This data is stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud (if enabled) to provide you with the app's core functionality
- **Storage:** All data is stored on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to this data.

### Usage Data
- **What we collect:** The app uses UserDefaults to store user preferences such as week start day, notification settings, and daily check-in preferences
- **How we use it:** To remember your app settings and preferences
- **Storage:** Stored locally on your device

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect any analytics or tracking data
- We do not collect personally identifiable information
- We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
- We do not share any data with third parties
- We do not display advertisements

## iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync in your device settings:
- Your routine items, tasks, and history entries are synced across your Apple devices using your personal iCloud account
- This data is encrypted in transit and stored in your iCloud account
- We do not have access to your iCloud data
- Apple's privacy policy applies to data stored in iCloud

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account
- We use standard iOS security features to protect your data
- No data is transmitted to our servers because we don't operate any servers

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete all app data by uninstalling the app or using the "Clear History" feature in Settings
- Control location permissions through your device settings
- Disable iCloud sync through your device settings

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the following Apple services:
- **CoreLocation:** For determining your geographic location
- **MapKit:** For address autocomplete suggestions
- **iCloud:** For syncing your data across devices (optional)
- **UserNotifications:** For sending scheduled reminders

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## Data Retention

- Data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
- iCloud data is retained according to your iCloud storage settings
- We do not retain any data on external servers

## California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used. As stated above, we only collect location data (stored locally) and user-generated content (stored locally and in your iCloud account). We do not sell personal information.

## International Users

The app stores data locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account. Data storage location is determined by your iCloud settings and Apple's data center locations.

---

**Summary:** .manifest is a privacy-focused app. Your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We don't collect analytics, we don't use trackers, and we don't operate any servers that store your data.
