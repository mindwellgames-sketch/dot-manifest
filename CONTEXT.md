2. Stop coding at 8pm for family time
3. Bedtime routine by 9:30pm (in bed by 10pm)

### CRITICAL MESSAGING NOTE
The user correctly pointed out that "Fill Your Cup" messaging is **backwards** - he's trying to STOP filling his cup with tasks. He needs to protect the emptiness, the rest, the boundaries. The app messaging now says:
- "Protect Your Boundaries" (not "Fill Your Cup")
- "Did you protect your boundaries?" (not "How full is your cup today?")
- "Your boundary progress" (not "Your cup filling progress")

**This distinction is crucial** - it's about creating space and rest, not adding more.
