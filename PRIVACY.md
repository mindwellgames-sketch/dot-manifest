# .manifest Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 29, 2025

## Overview

.manifest is a personal values and routine tracking app that respects your privacy. We believe your personal data should stay personal.

## Data Collection

.manifest stores the following data locally on your device:

- Your personal values and active value selections
- Daily routines and schedule items
- Tasks and appointments
- History of completed activities
- Location data (used only to calculate sunrise/sunset times for display)

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults).

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is synchronized across your devices using Apple's secure iCloud Key-Value Store. This data is encrypted by Apple and only accessible to you.

## Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services.

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit your personal data to any third parties. Your data never leaves your device except for optional iCloud sync to your own Apple account.

## Location Data

.manifest requests location permission solely to display sunrise and sunset gradient colors that reflect your local time of day. Location data is:

- Processed entirely on your device
- Never transmitted to external servers
- Never stored permanently (only cached temporarily for display purposes)

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

- **Export:** You can export all your data at any time through Settings → Backup & Restore
- **Delete:** You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app or through Settings → Reset Data
- **Revoke Permissions:** You can revoke location or notification permissions at any time in iOS Settings

## Children's Privacy

.manifest does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences focused on personal development.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
