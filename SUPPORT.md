## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add a new value?

Go to the **Values** tab and tap the **+** button in the top right corner. You can choose from our library of values or create a custom one.

### How do I set up my daily routine?

Go to the **Routines** tab and tap **+** to add routine items. You can set specific times and enable notifications for each item.

### How do I enable notifications?

When adding a routine item or task, toggle the notification switch on. Make sure you've granted notification permissions to .manifest in your iOS Settings.

### Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync in Settings, your data will also sync across your Apple devices.

### How do I backup my data?

Go to **Settings → Backup & Restore → Export Backup**. This creates a JSON file you can save or share.

### How do I restore from a backup?

Go to **Settings → Backup & Restore → Import Backup** and select your backup file.

### Can I delete all my data?

Yes. Go to **Settings** and use the reset options to clear your data. Alternatively, uninstalling the app removes all local data.
